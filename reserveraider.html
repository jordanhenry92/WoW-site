Reserve Raider

This person is not currently meeting the minimum standards
to be considered a raider but has demonstrated they want to
improve and has made an effort to do so. People assigned
this rank are encouraged to seek the help of officers and
raiders to identify and fix their specific issues to quickly
resolve them.
