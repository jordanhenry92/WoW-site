Trial

Trials are new members to the dream team. Please remember
to welcome them and make them feel like they have found
the best guild in WoW! The trial period will last 4 to 6
raids at the discretion of the officers. At the end of the
trial period officers will use the raider guidelines to
decide how to proceed. If trials are not a good fit for
the group only due to raid performance reasons, they will
be offered to stay on as casual members, otherwise they
will be removed from the guild.
