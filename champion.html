Champion

This rank is intended for the most dedicated and successful
members of the raid team who have gone well above and beyond
reasonable raider expectations and set a consistently positive
example in every aspect. In recognition of their nearly
perfect attendance and consistently exceptional performance
in raids, Champions will be given the highest priority on
raid invites, guild repairs, and flasks.
